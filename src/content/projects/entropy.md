## ENTROPY (2027)

A creative project under **Thoughtful App Co.** — *ENTROPY* is a short anime-style
film made as an experiment in agentic filmmaking: the first AI agentic Japanese
anime. It pairs a hand-shaped story and art direction with generative video,
audio, and editing pipelines driven by AI agents rather than a traditional
production crew.

## The experiment

*ENTROPY* asks what a film looks like when the pipeline itself is agentic — when
generation, iteration, and assembly are delegated to AI agents under creative
direction. The goal isn't to remove the human; it's to keep the human at the
helm of taste and story while the agents handle the frame-by-frame labor.

## Why it sits with Thoughtful App Co.

Thoughtful App Co. builds *for humans first* — and that thesis extends past
software into how we make creative work. *ENTROPY* is a probe into that question:
can agentic tooling expand what a small team (or one person) can author, without
surrendering authorship itself?
